About Kite Club
Koh Phangan

Kite Club Koh Phangan is located right on Thong Sala Beach — the heart of kitesurfing on Koh Phangan. A reef-protected flat water lagoon with shallow standing depth creates perfect beginner conditions.

The high season from January to April brings consistent south-east winds, with March and April offering some of the best rideable days.
